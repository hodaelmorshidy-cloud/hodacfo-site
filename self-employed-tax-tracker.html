    Common Questions

    How is this different from the Freelance Agency Finance System?

    This is a tax-focused tool for individuals managing their own quarterly estimates and multi-stream income. The Freelance Agency Finance System is a full operations toolkit for agency owners who also need pipeline, time tracking, invoicing, and client management.

    How is this different from TurboTax or H&R Block?

    TurboTax and H&R Block are filing tools—they prepare and submit your tax return after the year is over. This tracker is a planning tool—it helps you estimate what you owe during the year to make accurate quarterly payments and avoid underpayment penalties. Many freelancers use both.

    Do I still need an accountant?

    Yes. This tool helps you plan and track, but it does not file your taxes. Consider it your pre-accountant preparation system—it ensures you arrive at tax season with organized, accurate data.

    Is this specific to the 2026 tax year?

    Yes, rates and thresholds are set for 2026. For multi-year tracking, you would start a new workbook each year.

    Can I use this if I have both W-2 and 1099 income?

    Yes, that’s exactly the use case. The tracker handles both income types and calculates your combined estimated tax liability.
