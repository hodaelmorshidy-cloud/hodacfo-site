Privacy Policy

Overview

Hoda Finance (hodacfo.com) is operated by Hoda Elmorshidy, a Financial Controller and Fractional CFO based in Dubai, UAE. This policy explains what data is collected when you visit this site or purchase a product.

What Data Is Collected

This site collects the following:

  • Email address — only if you voluntarily subscribe to the email list via the forms on this site. Your email is stored in MailerLite and used only to deliver the promised resource and occasional relevant updates.
  • Anonymous analytics — page views and referral sources are tracked via Plausible Analytics. Plausible collects no personal data and uses no cookies. No IP addresses are stored.
  • Purchase data — when you buy a product, the transaction is processed by Gumroad. Your payment information and order details are handled by Gumroad under their own privacy policy. This site does not receive or store payment card information.

How Your Email Is Used

If you subscribe to the email list, you will receive the resource offered at sign-up and occasional emails relevant to finance tools and methodology. You can unsubscribe at any time using the link at the bottom of any email. Subscriber data is never sold or shared with third parties.

Third-Party Services

  • Gumroad — product delivery and payment processing. Gumroad Privacy Policy
  • MailerLite — email list management. MailerLite Privacy Policy
  • Plausible Analytics — anonymous site analytics. No cookies, no personal data. Plausible Privacy Policy

Cookies

This site does not use tracking cookies. Plausible Analytics is cookieless by design. If you subscribe via MailerLite, a session cookie may be set by MailerLite's form to handle the subscription process.

Your Rights

You may request deletion of your email data at any time by unsubscribing from the list or by emailing Hoda.elmorshidy@gmail.com. For Gumroad purchase data, refer to Gumroad's privacy policy directly.
